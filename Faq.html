What is GIGI Buys Homes?

GIGI Buys Homes is a real estate service dedicated to providing homeowners with a simple, fast, and stress-free way to sell their homes for cash.

How does the process work?

Our process is straightforward. Get in touch, and we’ll schedule a home evaluation, followed by a fair, no-obligation cash offer.

What types of properties does GIGI Buys Homes buy?

We buy a wide range of properties, including single-family homes, townhouses, condos, and more.

How do I get started with GIGI Buys Homes?

You can get started by contacting us through our website, phone, email, or live chat. Our team is here to help.

How quickly can I expect to receive an offer?

In most cases, you’ll receive an offer within 24-48 hours after the home evaluation.

Is there any obligation to accept GIGI Buys Homes' offer?

No, there’s no obligation. Our cash offer is completely transparent and comes with no pressure.

Are there any fees or hidden costs?

No, there are absolutely no fees or hidden costs. The offer we make is the amount you’ll receive.

How is the offer amount determined?

Our team evaluates your property to determine a fair market offer based on its condition and location.

What payment methods are available?

We offer various payment methods, including secure wire transfer or certified funds, for your convenience.

Do I need to make repairs or renovations before selling?

No, you don’t need to make any repairs or renovations. We buy homes as-is.

What should I expect during the home evaluation?

Our team will assess your home’s condition and unique features to provide an accurate valuation.

Can I sell my house as-is?

Yes, we buy homes as-is, so you don’t have to worry about repairs or improvements.

Can I choose my own closing date?

Yes, we offer flexible closing dates to accommodate your schedule.

How long does the closing process take?

Once you accept our offer, the closing process can be completed in as little as a week.

Is my personal information secure with GIGI Buys Homes?

Absolutely. We handle your personal information with the utmost security and privacy.

How does GIGI Buys Homes protect my data?

Our team follows strict privacy practices and uses advanced security measures to protect your data.

Will my information be shared with any third parties?

No, we do not share your information with third parties without your consent.

What areas does GIGI Buys Homes serve?

We operate in multiple regions across the U.S. Contact us to see if we serve your area.

Does GIGI Buys Homes operate outside the U.S.?

No, currently we operate only within the U.S.

How can I check if GIGI Buys Homes buys homes in my area?

Contact us directly to find out if we’re currently purchasing homes in your area.

Can GIGI Buys Homes help if my property has a pending mortgage renewal?

Yes, we can work with properties in a variety of financial situations, including pending mortgage renewals.

What if my property has unpermitted work or requires major repairs?

We buy homes as-is, so unpermitted work or major repairs are not an issue.

Does GIGI Buys Homes buy homes in foreclosure or with liens?

Yes, we can assist with homes facing foreclosure or properties with liens.

How can I contact GIGI Buys Homes for more information or to get started?

You can reach us through our website form, by calling our hotline, via email, or by live chat.

What if I have more questions not covered in the FAQ?

Feel free to reach out to our team. We’re here to answer any additional questions.

Does GIGI Buys Homes provide resources or advice for sellers?

Yes, we offer resources and advice to help guide sellers through the process.
